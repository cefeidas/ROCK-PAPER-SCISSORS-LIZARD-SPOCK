ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS, LIZARD, SPOCK



This is an online version of the game: ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS, LIZARD, SPOCK.

If you are unfamiliar with the rules of the game, please watch this tutorial:





You will compete against the computer in a series of matches. Each match consists of 5 rounds, and you must score higher than your opponent to win the match. Victory in a match will lead to a stronger opponent.

If you manage to defeat three opponents in a row, you will win the game. However, a single loss will result in losing all your progress.

When you're prepared, click the Start button to begin the game.
